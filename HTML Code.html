Hibiscus Flower

The Hibiscus flower has 679 different species, they come in many different sizes,colors,and shapes. Each flower does not bloom for very long but there are always new bus opening for a continuations show of color. 

Even tho there are over 300 species of this flower Hibiscus Sabdariffa  holds a special place in the ayurvedic herbs. These flowers are known for growing Asia and North America, along with Haiti, Malaysia and South Korea.

These flowers are to be watered regularly, especially in hot weather. They are to be kept in moist and well drained soil and the PH of the soil must be acidic or neutral.This is an Annual and Perennial plant depending on what species you have.These plants can range from 3-10ft tall and 2-8ft wide.Keep them in full sun and or partial shade for best growth.

These flowers bloom in the summer and fall time frame,but bloom year round in tropical climes. They can come in the colors of white,red, pink, yellow, and orange.
